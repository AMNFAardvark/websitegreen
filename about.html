About FamilyVest

Built on Experience.
Driven by Purpose.

FamilyVest exists because financial advice should be built around your life, not around products. We're a fee-only fiduciary practice providing comprehensive financial planning and investment management. No commissions, no conflicts, no hidden agendas. Todd and Elliot work as a team so you have maximum coverage and availability.

Todd Sensing

Founder & Lead Advisor · CFA, CFP®

Todd founded FamilyVest to deliver the kind of financial advice he'd want for his own family: complete, coordinated, and free of conflicts. As a CFA charterholder and CFP® professional, he brings institutional-grade investment expertise to families and individuals who want more than a stock picker. They want a partner who understands the full picture: tax strategy, retirement planning, estate coordination, insurance, and how all those pieces interact.

Todd's planning philosophy is shaped by personal experience as much as professional credentials. As the father of two sons with autism, he understands the financial complexities that families with special needs face, from preserving benefit eligibility to structuring trusts to funding lifetime care. That lived experience drives FamilyVest's deep expertise in special needs planning, one of the firm's core differentiators.

Based along Florida's Emerald Coast, FamilyVest serves clients in the Destin/30A corridor and nationwide.
