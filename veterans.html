Financial Planning for Veterans & Military Families

You served with discipline and purpose. Your financial plan should reflect the same values. We help veterans and military families navigate the unique benefits, transitions, and planning opportunities that come with service.

Schedule a Conversation

What We Help Veterans Navigate

Military Retirement & Pension Planning

Coordinating military pension, TSP distributions, and Social Security timing for maximum lifetime income.

VA Benefits Coordination

Understanding how VA disability, education benefits, and healthcare interact with your broader financial plan.

TSP to IRA Rollover Strategy

When to roll over, when to keep TSP, and how to optimize the transition for tax efficiency and investment flexibility.

Transition & Second Career Planning

Financial planning for the transition from active duty to civilian life, including income replacement and career change budgeting.

Survivor & Family Protection

SBP elections, SGLI/VGLI conversion decisions, and estate planning to protect your family if something happens.

Special Needs & Disability Planning

For veterans with service-connected disabilities or dependents with special needs, we coordinate benefits with trusts, ABLE accounts, and long-term care planning.

Why Veterans Choose FamilyVest

We are a fiduciary firm. That means we are legally required to act in your best interest. No commissions, no product sales, no conflicts. Just straightforward advice from someone who respects your service and understands the financial complexity that comes with it.

Our founder has deep experience in special needs planning, which gives us a unique lens on VA disability coordination and benefit preservation that most advisors miss.
