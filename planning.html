How We Plan

A process designed to evolve with your life.

Planning at FamilyVest is not a binder you receive and forget. It is an ongoing cycle that adapts as your circumstances change.

1

Discovery Conversation

We listen. You share what matters most: your goals, concerns, family situation, and financial complexity. No cost, no obligation. We determine if we are the right fit.

2

Data Gathering & Analysis

We collect the full picture: accounts, tax returns, insurance policies, estate documents, benefits. Then we run the analysis to identify gaps, risks, and opportunities.

3

Plan Development

We build your comprehensive plan: coordinated recommendations across tax, retirement, estate, insurance, investments, and cash flow. Everything connected.

4

Presentation & Investment Recommendations

We walk you through the plan in detail, answer questions, and make adjustments. You will have a living plan with the ability to run your own scenario analysis.

5

Implementation

We execute: opening accounts, repositioning investments, coordinating with your CPA and attorney. No loose ends.

6

Ongoing Partnership

This is where most advisors stop and we keep going. Regular reviews. Proactive tax planning. Real-time adjustments when life changes. Unlimited access whenever you need us.
